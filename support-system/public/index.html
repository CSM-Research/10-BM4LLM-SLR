BM4LLM-SLR

LLMs to Support Studies Selection in Systematic Literature Reviews

You are about to participate in a voluntary experiment conducted by the lead researcher, Vinicius dos Santos that is investigating the usage of Large Language Models (LLM) in SLR process. More especifically, in the primary studies selection task.

Important: This session will be recorded for transcription and artifact improvement purposes. Transcriptions may be published for scientific analysis, but audio recordings will not be shared. By participating, you agree to share your oppinions and impressions recorded textually.

Your impressions and feedback on the artifact are highly valuable to us. By clicking "I agree", you confirm that you understand and accept these terms.
